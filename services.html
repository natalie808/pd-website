Our Services

At Pocket Docs Billing LLC, we offer a comprehensive suite of services to streamline your practice's administrative and technical operations. We are dedicated to maximizing your revenue and minimizing your hassles.

Medical Billing

Our expert medical billing services ensure timely and accurate claim processing, helping you improve cash flow and reduce billing errors. We handle:

  • Claims submission and diligent follow-up.
  • Accurate payment posting and reconciliation.
  • Proactive denial management and appeals.
  • Efficient patient statement processing and inquiries.

Credentialing & Recredentialing

Navigating the complexities of provider credentialing is crucial. We manage the entire process to ensure your providers are properly enrolled and compliant. Our services include:

  • Comprehensive provider credentialing and re-credentialing.
  • Managing insurance network applications and enrollments.
  • Maintenance of essential licenses and certifications.

EHR Set up & Tech Support

We provide technical support for leading EHR systems to ensure your practice operates smoothly. We specialize in AdvancedMD, Charm Health, Practice Fusion, Simple Practice, and Elation. Our support includes:

  • Setting up your clinic within the EHR system.
  • Adding and managing provider accounts as needed.
  • Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) enrollment assistance.
